Absolutely free! We will arrange a professional team with precision tools to measure your property on-site at no charge whatsoever.

Depending on the property size and complexity, typical timelines are: residential units 60–90 days, penthouses 90–120 days, and villa projects 6–12 months. A detailed schedule will be provided once the design plan is finalized.
