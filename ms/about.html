Shanghai Jie Shang Decoration Group

Shanghai Jie Shang Decoration Group is a large-scale private joint-stock comprehensive enterprise integrating interior and exterior design & decoration, road construction, building construction, advertising & media, and catering & hospitality. The company holds a National Class II Decoration Qualification, has been awarded the AAAAA Credit Enterprise designation, and is a member of the Shanghai Decoration Industry Association.

As a pioneer of modern Chinese home culture since its establishment in 2003, Jie Shang has consistently pursued a specialized boutique development strategy, executing the highest standards throughout the entire process. Adhering to a group management model, the company guides the orderly growth of all its branches.

The company specializes in full-service design and construction for apartments, villas, and commercial spaces. Leveraging its group management model and the boundless creativity and passion of its cutting-edge designers, Jie Shang leads a trendy, natural, and comfortable lifestyle—delivering a one-stop VIP home decoration experience for successful individuals both domestically and internationally.

Jie Shang employs over 1,500 management and technical professionals. The company has forged strategic alliances with numerous Fortune 500 material suppliers worldwide, all to perfectly realize the dream of custom private residences.
