COMPANY PROFILE

Shanghai Jie Shang Decoration Group

Shanghai Jie Shang Decoration Group is a large-scale private joint-stock comprehensive enterprise integrating interior and exterior design & decoration, road construction, building construction, advertising & media, and catering & hospitality. The company holds a National Class II Decoration Qualification, has been awarded the AAAAA Credit Enterprise designation, and is a member of the Shanghai Decoration Industry Association.

As a pioneer of modern Chinese home culture since its establishment in 2003, Jie Shang has consistently pursued a specialized boutique development strategy, executing the highest standards throughout the entire process. Adhering to a group management model, the company guides the orderly growth of all its branches.

The company specializes in full-service design and construction for apartments, villas, and commercial spaces. Leveraging its group management model and the boundless creativity and passion of its cutting-edge designers, Jie Shang leads a trendy, natural, and comfortable lifestyle—delivering a one-stop VIP home decoration experience for successful individuals both domestically and internationally.

Jie Shang employs over 1,500 management and technical professionals. The company has forged strategic alliances with numerous Fortune 500 material suppliers worldwide, all to perfectly realize the dream of custom private residences.

OUR ADVANTAGES

Core Advantages

01

In-House Team, No Subcontracting

All construction personnel are full-time employees who have undergone rigorous training and assessment. No subcontracting or outsourcing ensures controllable quality and schedule. Project managers are on-site throughout, with dedicated personnel responsible for inspecting each process.

02

Transparent Pricing, No Hidden Costs

We sign fixed-price contracts—what's quoted is what you pay. All items are clearly priced with material brands, models, and specifications listed in detail. Without client-requested changes, no additional costs will be added, ensuring a worry-free renovation.

03

Eco-Friendly Materials, Healthy Homes

We carefully select environmentally certified materials, with test reports provided for all primary materials. Strategic partnerships with renowned domestic and international building material brands ensure quality at competitive prices, making your home healthier and safer.

04

Standardized Craftsmanship, Precision Construction

We have established a standardized construction process system, with detailed construction standards and acceptance criteria for plumbing, electrical, masonry, carpentry, and painting. Advanced tools and management systems ensure consistent engineering quality.

05

Comprehensive After-Sales, Lifetime Maintenance

Extended warranty periods: 5 years for basic works, 10 years for waterproofing, 50 years for plumbing, and lifetime warranty for electrical wiring. A dedicated after-sales team responds promptly to all repair requests.

06

Local Enterprise, Better Understanding of You

As a Shanghai-based company, we have a deeper understanding of local clients' needs and habits. From design style preferences to lifestyle considerations, from climate characteristics to building structures, we deliver more thoughtful, customized solutions.
